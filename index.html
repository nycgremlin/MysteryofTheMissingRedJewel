The Mystery of The Missing Red Jewel

By Alexander Zautashvili

A choose your own adventure mystery game!

"Synthetic ruby 2" by James St. John is licensed under CC BY 2.0

You are Sam. A detective in the burglary unit patrolling the city on a murky night. Your partner Jimbo gets a call from the sheriff saying that someone stole the Abbey Diamond from the Pirates and Sailors Museum. The Abbey Diamond, named after the initial Countess it was gifted to, was later fought over for years by rival pirate gangs because of its blood red color and the hypnotic light that shines out of it. Nowadays, most people refer to it as the Red Jewel.

When you and Jimbo arrive at the scene, a rookie cop runs up and introduces himself as Zabel. Zabel informs you that they have secured three people who were definitely in the area when the Jewel was taken. There’s Old Man Frankenbeet, Jorge the Bodybuilder, and the world renowned Scientist Laura. Jimbo realizes that the Red Jewel was protected by high-grade polymer glass and instantly suspects the strongest suspect, Jorge the Bodybuilder. You know Jimbo is always quick to pick a suspect, but he is also usually right. There is also the fact that Scientist Laura’s obsession with studying the Red Jewel has been all over the papers. Meanwhile, Old Man Frankenbeet is apparently obsessed with owning the Red Jewel and was about to participate in a museum auction to procure it.

All of this debatable evidence has your head spinning and you aren’t sure if listening to the testimonies would be unbiased without further evidence. You decide to hear their testimonies later and look for clues now. Zabel tells you they have two possible leads:

A pawnshop who dropped a tip about a suspicious seller.
