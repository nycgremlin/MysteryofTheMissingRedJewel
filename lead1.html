The Mystery of The Missing Red Jewel

By Alexander Zautashvili

A choose your own adventure mystery game!

"Synthetic ruby 2" by James St. John is licensed under CC BY 2.0

On the way to the pawnshop, Zabel explains that he received a call from the owner about a shifty buyer he was going to meet. They never got to meet face to face because of price disputes over the item and strangely enough the item was gone the next day. Jimbo asks Zabel what any of this has to do with the case, Zabel flashes a grin and explains that the item was a special drill bit designed to break high-grade polymer glass without making a sound, the same kind of glass that was protecting the Red Jewel. You meet with the owner who says the person who wanted the drill bit was looking for a lower price because they had recently gotten into money trouble. Jimbo says he had Jorge the Bodybuilder pegged for breaking the glass. but this clue proves that any of the three are capable of doing so.
