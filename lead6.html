The Mystery of The Missing Red Jewel

By Alexander Zautashvili

A choose your own adventure mystery game!

"Synthetic ruby 2" by James St. John is licensed under CC BY 2.0

All of you valiantly dig through the dumpster. Zabel goes completely wide-eyed and you can tell he’s spotted something. Although it isn’t the Red Jewel, it is an important clue: an antique drill with a special diamond drill bit attached. You deduce it must’ve been used to break the polymer glass. You examine the drill for clues and notice how worn it is, almost like it wasn’t meant to be used anymore, but rather was in an exhibition before. When you ask the museum curator, he confirms that the drill actually was part of their "Origin of Home Appliances" exhibit. He also adds that it was sold at a private auction, but he’s not sure who specifically bought it. You all decide to head back to the station to choose a suspect from the lineup.
