The Mystery of The Missing Red Jewel

By Alexander Zautashvili

A choose your own adventure mystery game!

"Synthetic ruby 2" by James St. John is licensed under CC BY 2.0

Zabel says the suspects are getting annoyed for being in holding for so long. You wonder if one of them is annoyed because they won’t have a chance to stash the Red Jewel. Zabel informs you that you can only hold the suspects for 24 hours, so you decide to hurry up and get more specific with the investigation.

Jimbo and Zabel make their suggestions:

Jimbo says he wants to drive to Scientist Laura’s lab, because for some reason, he’s convinced she must’ve stolen it. You know you must be completely sure when you pick because your reputation as a detective is on the line, so you need hard proof, but then again, Jimbo is just so darn lucky.

"Student in lab" by UGA CAES/Extension is licensed under CC BY-NC 2.0

Zabel has a sort of zany lead in mind, but it is definitely specific to a suspect. He wants to visit Old Man Frankenbeet’s bank, the one he has been a member of for over 45 years. Zabel says that is where he would store the Red Jewel if he could.
