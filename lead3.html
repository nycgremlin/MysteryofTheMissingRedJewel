The Mystery of The Missing Red Jewel

By Alexander Zautashvili

A choose your own adventure mystery game!

"Synthetic ruby 2" by James St. John is licensed under CC BY 2.0

You decide to trust Jimbo’s gut, despite the recent turn it has taken. As you drive up, you notice that no one is in the lab because it’s the weekend. This makes it easy to search Scientist Laura’s office and luckily, Jimbo ends up finding something relevant to the Red Jewel. It’s a research log that details exactly what experiments the Scientist intends to perform on the Red Jewel. Jimbo is focused on this part and is convinced that this is hard proof of Scientist Laura’s guilt. The research log is long, and has a specific plan at the end detailing Scientist Laura’s plan to buy the Red Jewel at the museum auction. It continues to state that through some independent research of her own, she found out that the Old Man was having trouble pooling enough money for the Red Jewel. You take note that Old Man Frankenbeet was having financial troubles leading up to this burglary.
